Pancakes

Easy to make and delicious, here is how to make our pancakes!

Everyone loves pancakes, they are exactly what you want in the morning and with our simple recipe you will have them sorted in less than 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of Flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Stir together the flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add the egg, milk and melted butter into the mix
  3. Heat a griddle or pan to a medium high heat, add oil
  4. Pour on some batter and flip when bubbles appear on the top
  5. Repeat until all are finished
  6. Serve with butter, maple syrup or even bacon!
