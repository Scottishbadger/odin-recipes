Fajitas

Enjoy a taste of Mexico with our easy fajita recipe!

Chicken Fajitas are a lovely meal made with an easy recipe to boot! Delicious chicken, spices and vegetables are fried together then placed in wraps with a topping of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • 1 White Onion
  • 2 Bell peppers
  • A packet of spice mix
  • A packet of wraps

Steps

  1. Fry the chicken for 6-8 minutes, until browned
  2. Remove chicken from frying pan and place aside
  3. Slice your peppers and onion then fry them until soft
  4. Add chicken in with fried vegeabtles
  5. Add in spice mix, stir together
  6. Take off heat, serve with wraps
